About Us

The Greenwood Community Library has been a cornerstone of our community since its establishment in 1950. Our mission is to provide a welcoming environment where everyone can explore, learn, and connect. We offer a wide range of resources, including books, digital media, and community programs to support lifelong learning and enrichment.

Our library is committed to promoting literacy, fostering a love for reading, and providing access to information for all ages. We host regular events such as story times, book clubs, and educational workshops to engage and inspire our patrons.

We invite you to visit our library, meet our dedicated staff, and take advantage of the many services and resources we offer. Together, we can cultivate a vibrant and informed community.
