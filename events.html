Upcoming Events

Join us for our exciting events at Greenwood Community Library. We offer a variety of programs for all ages, including story times, book clubs, author talks, workshops, and more. Our events are designed to engage, educate, and inspire our community.

  • Story Time: Every Wednesday at 10:00 AM - Join us for a fun and interactive story time session for children aged 3-7.
  • Book Club: First Thursday of every month at 6:30 PM - Discuss the book of the month with fellow readers.
  • Author Talk: June 15th at 2:00 PM - Meet local author Jane Doe and learn about her latest book.
  • Workshop: June 20th at 3:00 PM - "Introduction to Digital Photography" - Learn the basics of digital photography.
  • Summer Reading Kickoff: July 1st at 11:00 AM - Celebrate the start of our summer reading program with fun activities, games, and a special guest appearance by children's author Alice Brown.

Stay tuned for more events and updates. We look forward to seeing you at the library!
